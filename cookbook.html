Cheesy Ham and Potatoe Soup

This cheesy potatoe soup is super easy to make and awesome for a cold day (especially those e-learning days when you). There is no real prep involved other than opening up some bages and throwing the ingredients in. No chopping or measuring required, just open them up and in the crock-pot they go!

Ingredient

Quantity
Potatoes O'Brien with Onions and Peppers 2 bags
(28oz each)
Vegtible Broth 3 cans
(14.5oz each)
Cream of Celary Soup 2 cans
(10.5oz each)
Cream Cheese (softened) 2 packages
(8oz each)
Diced Ham packages
(8oz each)
Shredded Cheese optional

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients except for the cream cheese and shredded cheese into a large crock pot.

  2. Mix well and cook on low for 6 hours.

  3. Add the cream cheese and continue to cook for 45 minutes.

  4. Stir well and spoon into bowls. Add shredded cheee if desired.

Homemade Southern French Dressing

One of my favorite salad dressings growing up was this stuff called Dorthy Lynch. It is very similar to a french dressing but it has some extra tangyness to it. Because I moved around a lot it was sometimes hard to find that dressing so I figured out how to make it myself at home. I think that this goes great on just a simple salad is pairs well with the potatoe soup above. Enjoy!

Ingredient

Quantity
Potatoes O'Brien with Onions and Peppers 2 bags
(28oz each)
Ketchup/td> 1 1/2 cups
Vegetable Oil 1 cup
White Sugar 1/4 cup
White Vinegar 1/4 cup
Yellow Mustard 1 tsp
Celery Seeds 1 tsp
Cracked Pepper 1/4 tsp
Garlic Powder 1/8 tsp

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients into a large container with a lid and mix well.

  2. If the dressing is very thin, stir in about 1/4 tsp of cornstarch.

  3. Seal the container and refregerate it for several hours. (It may be served immediatly but chilling it brings out the flavors more)
