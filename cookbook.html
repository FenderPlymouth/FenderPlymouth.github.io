Ingredient

Quantity
Potatoes O'Brien with Onions and Peppers 2 bags
(28oz each)
Vegtible Broth 3 cans
(14.5oz each)
Cream of Celary Soup 2 cans
(10.5oz each)
Cream Cheese (softened) 2 packages
(8oz each)
Diced Ham packages
(8oz each)
Shredded Cheese optional

Instructions

  1. Combine all the ingredients except for the cream cheese and shredded cheese into a large crock pot.

  2. Mix well and cook on low for 6 hours.

  3. Add the cream cheese and continue to cook for 45 minutes.

  4. Stir well and spoon into bowls. Add shredded cheee if desired.
